Riyu Yasunaga (安永 璃優 Yasunaga Riyu) is an original character set in the universe of the Shōjo☆Kageki Revue Starlight franchise. She is one of the elite students at Siegfeld Institute of Music with the title of "Frau Jade", which belonged to Shiori Yumeoji the previous year. Together with Moka Izumi, they form the "next generation" of edels filling in the space after Akira Yukishiro and Michiru Otori's retirement.
